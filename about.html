About Me

Hi! I'm Raymond, and I graduated from UC Santa Barbara with a B.S. double major in Statistics & Data Science and Psychological and Brain Sciences. As someone who grew up living abroad for a good amount of my childhood, I developed an interest in learning about different cultures. During college, I became fascinated by the idea of pursuing this interest by harnessing real-world data to better understand people, which led me down the path of data science and analysis. I am especially passionate about utilizing my skills and exploring my interests in the entertainment industry because I believe entertainment has the unique ability to allow people to connect with each other by knocking down cultural barriers and dissolving any differences in people's backgrounds. Think about the movie Parasite: How was it such an international success despite being made by a small country in Asia? In my free time, I like to play sports and video games, watch anime, sing, and also find new music to listen to! Feel free to look through my projects!
